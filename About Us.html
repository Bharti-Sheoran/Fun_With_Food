About Us:

'Fun With Food' is a web site that provides folks to explore all kinds of famous and traditional dishes of each state. This site, not only includes dishes, but their recipes also. It becomes easy for the people to find the dishes and recipes at one web site. This page is made by two students after seeing the interest of folks in food and also observing the difficulty of people who are not able to find the recipe and dish on a single web site.

Nowadays, people love to visit different places and wants to enjoy the special dishes of that places. So, this web site will help them in finding the food of their taste and style. It includes a variety of dishes, such as desserts, beverages etc. of every state.

Most importantly, people of all age groups can use web page because of the unique and special taste of each state. For instance, folks of young age can try spicy dishes, infants can have desserts of their taste and elderly age group enjoy the traditional food. In this way, everyone is in benefit from this web site.

Most important thing that we kept in mind while making this web site is that people can fully show their trust in it. Nothing illegal work is done by this site. This web site makes the people sure about their interest in our web site. This page helps them to find the interest in cooking and eating of traditional dishes. In this way, many people come to known about the traditional dishes of our country and known the fact that how precious this treasure is.
