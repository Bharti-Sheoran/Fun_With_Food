Haryana

Haryana is one of the 29 states in India, located in the northern part of the country. The official language of Haryana is Hindi.Haryana has its own unique traditional folk music, folk dances, saang (folk theatre), cinema, belief system such as Jathera (ancestral worship), and arts such as Phulkari and Shisha embroidery.Haryanvi musical folk theatre main types are Saang, Rasa lila and Ragini.As per a survey, 81% people of Haryana are vegetarian, and the regional cuisine features the staples of roti, saag, vegetarian sabzi and milk products such as ghee, milk, lassi and kheer.

Kachri ki chutney

Kachri is a wild variety of cucumber that resembles a brown yellow small melon. When mixed with garlic, salt, red chilli powder, turmeric and coriander. Recipe Servings: 3 \ Prep Time: 02 mins \ Cook Time: 30 mins \ Total Cook Time: 32 mins \ Difficulty Level: Easy \

Ingredients

  • 50 gm kachri
  • 100 gm garlic paste
  • A pinch of turmeric powder
  • 10 gm red chilli powder
  • 10 gm coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 gm cumin seeds
  • A pinch of asafoetida
  • Oil

How to make Kachri ki chutney

  • Soak the kachri for 5-6 hours and grind it to a fine paste.
  • Then mix well the kachri paste, garlic, salt, red chilli powder, turmeric, coriander powder.
  • Take oil in a pan.
  • Add the cumin seeds and let them crackle.
  • Add the asafoetida as well as the kachri mixture to it.
  • Add around 60 ml of lukewarm water.
  • Let it simmer for 4-5 minutes and remove from fire.
  • Note: Instead of pre packed red chilli powder, freshly ground red chillies give a far better flavour.

Bajra Aloo Roti

Millet flour kneaded with boiled potatoes, some masalas and rolled out into flavourful rotis.
Prep Time : 11-15 minutes \Cook time : 11-15 minutes \ Serve : 4 \ Level Of Cooking : Easy \ Taste : Mild

Ingredients

  • Millet flour (bajre ka atta) 1 1/4 cups
  • Potatoes boiled and peeled 2 medium
  • Salt to taste
  • Carom seeds (ajwain) 1 teaspoon
  • Coriander powder 1/2 teaspoon
  • Cumin powder 1/2 teaspoon
  • Red chilli powder 1/2 teaspoon

How to make Bajra aloo roti

  • Mix bajra atta, salt, carom seeds, coriander powder, cumin powder and red chillii powder in a bowl.
  • Grate potatoes into it. Add enough hot water and knead into a medium soft dough.
  • Heat a non stick tawa. Divide the dough into equal portions and roll into balls.
  • Flatten them and spread into small thick rotis.
  • Place them on the tawa and cook on medium heat.
  • Serve hot or warm with a chutney of your choice.

Aloo ka halwa

Known to be made during festivals like Ekadashi, Aloo Ka Halwa (potato pudding) is made of boiled and mashed potatoes and pure ghee which adds to its wonderful flavour and aroma.
Recipe Servings: 4 \ Prep Time: 05 mins \ Cook Time: 15 mins \ Total Cook Time: 20 mins \ Difficulty Level: Easy \

Ingredients

  • 3 large potatoes, boiled
  • 1 Tbsp ghee
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 Tbsp cashews chopped & lightly roasted
  • 1/4 Tbsp almonds flaked or chopped
  • A pinch of cardamom powder
  • 1/2 Tbsp raisins

How to make Aloo ka halwa

  • Boil the potatoes and peel off the skin. Once, done mash it with your hands.
  • In a pan, pour some ghee and let it heat. Then add the mashed potatoes and let it roast for 1-2 minutes
  • Add milk, sugar and raisins to the potatoes and mix them well.
  • Cook for another 8-10 minutes and stir frequently.
  • Add cardamom and stir.
  • Garnish with flaked almonds & cashews. Serve hot.
